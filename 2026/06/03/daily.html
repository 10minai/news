Publishers can now opt out of AI search in the UK

UK regulators force Google to offer an opt-out tool for generative AI search — starting in the UK, with plans for a global rollout.

Why it matters: Sets a precedent for publisher control over AI training data and search snippets.

Hermes Desktop: Open-source AI agent for every platform

Nous Research launches Hermes Desktop, an open-source AI agent that runs locally across Windows, macOS, and Linux.

Why it matters: Lowers the barrier for developers to build and deploy autonomous agents without cloud lock-in.
