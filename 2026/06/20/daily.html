  • Open-source legend builds real-time robot control layer

🥇 He made your free video player run smoothly. Now he’s doing that for robots

Jean-Baptiste Kempf, creator of VLC, has launched Kyber—a real-time infrastructure layer for remote robot control — the system aims to reduce latency and improve reliability for fleets of autonomous devices.

Why it matters: If Kyber delivers on its promise, it could become the “TCP/IP for robots,” standardizing how machines communicate and coordinate at scale.
