🥇 Mistral drops open-weight model matching GPT-4o

Mistral AI released Mistral-Nemo-2, an open-weight model outperforming GPT-4o on benchmarks — The model is available under a permissive license for commercial use, with weights published on Hugging Face.

Why it matters: Open-weight models are now competitive with proprietary frontrunners, accelerating enterprise adoption and customization.

EU AI Act enforcement details trigger backlash

The European Commission published final enforcement rules for the AI Act — High-risk systems must now undergo third-party audits before deployment, with fines up to 7% of global revenue.

Why it matters: The strict timeline and audit requirements could slow AI deployment in Europe, prompting calls for phased compliance.

Google DeepMind unveils 1.8T-parameter Gemini Ultra 2

Google DeepMind announced Gemini Ultra 2, a 1.8 trillion-parameter model with improved reasoning and multimodal capabilities — The model is currently in limited preview for select enterprise partners.

Why it matters: The parameter leap suggests scaling laws still hold, but training costs may limit access to well-funded labs.

Anthropic launches AI text detection tool

Anthropic released a free tool to detect AI-generated text with 90% accuracy — The classifier focuses on subtle linguistic patterns rather than watermarking.

Why it matters: Reliable detection could ease concerns about misinformation, though false positives remain a risk.
