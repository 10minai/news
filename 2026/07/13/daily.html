🥇 Mistral releases open-weight model outperforming Llama 3.1

Mistral’s new 45B-parameter model, Mathstral, surpasses Meta’s Llama 3.1 405B on math and coding tasks — The model is fully open-weight, licensed for commercial use, and trained on a mix of synthetic and public datasets.

Why it matters: Open-weight models are now competitive with frontier closed models, accelerating adoption in regulated industries.

EU AI Act enforcement begins with first fines

Two companies fined under the EU AI Act for deploying unregistered high-risk AI systems — One in healthcare diagnostics, the other in hiring automation. Fines total €12 million.

Why it matters: The EU’s enforcement is faster than expected, signaling a shift from policy to practice.

DeepMind’s agent solves unseen math problems

Google DeepMind’s AlphaProof agent achieves silver-medal performance on unseen International Math Olympiad problems — The system combines reinforcement learning with formal proof verification.

Why it matters: Agents are moving beyond pattern recognition to genuine problem-solving in novel domains.

Nvidia’s next-gen AI chip delayed

Nvidia’s B200 AI chip faces production delays due to supply chain bottlenecks — The chip, expected in Q4 2026, is now slated for early 2027.

Why it matters: Delays could slow AI infrastructure rollouts for cloud providers and enterprises.
