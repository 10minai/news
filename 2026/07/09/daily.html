🥇 Mistral releases Mixtral 8x22B-Instruct, claims top open-weight spot

Mistral’s Mixtral 8x22B-Instruct outperforms Meta’s Llama 3.1 405B in early third-party tests — The model uses a sparse mixture-of-experts architecture and is released under Apache 2.0 license.

Why it matters: The first credible open-weight challenger to Llama’s dominance could shift enterprise adoption away from proprietary APIs.

EU AI Act enforcement starts with fines for non-compliance

The European Commission issued its first penalties under the AI Act, targeting three companies for failing to disclose training data sources — Fines range from €2M to €12M, with 30-day cure periods.

Why it matters: The enforcement wave will test whether the Act’s transparency rules can survive legal challenges from Big Tech.

Google DeepMind trains 100B-parameter agent in simulated physics

DeepMind’s new “PhysiQ” agent solves complex rigid-body dynamics tasks in a custom simulator — The model uses reinforcement learning and a novel curriculum to generalize across unseen environments.

Why it matters: A step toward agents that can reason about real-world physics, not just language.

US tightens AI chip export rules to China

The Biden administration expanded restrictions on high-end AI chips, adding new licensing requirements for data-center GPUs — The move follows reports of Chinese firms bypassing earlier curbs via third-party resellers.

Why it matters: The escalation could accelerate China’s push for domestic alternatives, fragmenting the global AI hardware market.
