🥇 Mistral drops open-weight model matching GPT-4o performance

Mistral AI released Mixtral 4o, an open-weight model scoring 89.2 on MMLU — the first openly licensed model to reach GPT-4o-level benchmarks.

Why it matters: Open-weight models now compete with closed frontier systems, shifting the balance of power in AI development.

EU AI Act enforcement starts with fines for non-compliance

The European Commission issued its first penalties under the AI Act — two companies fined €12M and €8M for deploying unregistered high-risk systems.

Why it matters: Regulatory teeth are now real, forcing companies to audit models before EU deployment.

Google DeepMind open-sources robotics control stack

DeepMind released OpenRT-X, a modular framework for real-time robot control — combining vision, planning, and low-level motor commands in a single stack.

Why it matters: Standardized robotics software could accelerate adoption beyond research labs.

Anthropic launches $10M safety research grants

Anthropic opened applications for its new AI Safety Research Fund — targeting independent researchers studying alignment, interpretability, and societal impact.

Why it matters: Funding shifts from in-house teams to external voices, diversifying safety research.
