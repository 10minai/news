🥇 Mistral drops Mixtral 8x22B, strongest open-weight model to date

Mistral releases Mixtral 8x22B, claiming it outperforms Llama 3 70B on most benchmarks — The model uses a sparse mixture-of-experts architecture and is licensed under Apache 2.0.

Why it matters: Open-weight models are now competitive with closed alternatives, forcing incumbents to rethink pricing and access.

Nvidia accelerates Blackwell timeline, ships GB200 in Q3

Nvidia announces early shipments of GB200 Grace Blackwell chips, moving up the launch by two quarters — The move follows reports of AMD and Intel closing the performance gap.

Why it matters: Hardware margins could compress faster than expected if the AI chip monopoly faces real competition.

EU proposes “AI sovereignty” rules for government use

European Commission drafts regulations requiring public-sector AI systems to use EU-hosted models — The rules would apply to all member states starting in 2027.

Why it matters: Could fragment the AI market, forcing providers to maintain separate EU-only infrastructure.

Anthropic slashes Claude API prices by 30%

Anthropic reduces pricing for Claude 3.5 Sonnet API calls, matching GPT-4o’s cost per token — The move follows similar cuts from Google and Meta earlier this month.

Why it matters: API pricing wars are accelerating, but margins may not hold at these levels.
