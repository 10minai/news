🥇 Meta’s Llama 3.2 11B model tops open-weight benchmarks

Llama 3.2 11B outperforms larger open models on MMLU and coding tasks — Meta released the model under a permissive license, including weights for the first time in its 11B class.

Why it matters: Open-weight models are now competitive with closed 70B+ systems, shifting the balance for startups and researchers.

EU AI Act enforcement begins with three probes

EU regulators open investigations into undisclosed high-risk AI systems — Targets include a French insurer’s claims model and a German hiring tool, both flagged by civil society groups.

Why it matters: The first real-world test of the AI Act’s risk-tiering system could set precedents for global compliance.

Mistral launches commercial fine-tuning API

Mistral’s new API lets businesses fine-tune models on proprietary data — Pricing starts at $0.50 per million tokens, with a 50GB data cap per customer.

Why it matters: The first major API provider to offer fine-tuning without usage restrictions, lowering barriers for custom enterprise models.

US-China AI safety talks stall over export controls

Bilateral working group pauses after US tightens GPU export rules — China’s delegation walked out of the third round, citing “unilateral restrictions” on hardware.

Why it matters: The collapse of safety talks removes a rare channel for aligning on AI risks between the two largest AI powers.
