🥇 Mistral Large 3 outscores Llama 3.1 in leaked benchmarks

Mistral’s latest open-weight model edges out Meta’s Llama 3.1 in early internal tests — The 405B-parameter model shows gains in coding and multilingual tasks, though full details remain under wraps.

Why it matters: The first credible open-weight challenger to Llama’s dominance could shift the balance in enterprise adoption.

Nvidia’s B100 chips face production delays

Nvidia confirms its next-gen AI accelerators won’t ship until Q1 2027 — Supply chain bottlenecks and yield issues push back the Blackwell architecture’s debut by six months.

Why it matters: Cloud providers and startups may pivot to AMD or in-house alternatives, easing Nvidia’s near-monopoly.

EU AI Act enforcement kicks off with high-risk audits

The European Commission begins auditing AI systems in healthcare and finance — Companies must submit compliance documentation by September 1.

Why it matters: Early enforcement signals the EU’s seriousness about regulating AI, not just legislating it.

Anthropic launches AI text detection tool

Anthropic releases a free tool to identify AI-generated text — The classifier focuses on subtle linguistic patterns but admits a 15% false-positive rate.

Why it matters: Schools and publishers now have a vendor-backed alternative to OpenAI’s discontinued detector.
